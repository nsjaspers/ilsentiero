The Sanctuary

Sanctuary between
Land & Sea.

Right at the sea — nothing between us and the water but one small street. The terrace looks out over the Adriatic. Beautiful trees give shade. A little island in front breaks the waves, filling the air with that constant sound of the sea. Inside: warm colours, candles, books, a practice room. Fires in the evening. The sunrise different every single day. And Maya — our cat, who arrived in a storm and never left — who has a way of finding exactly who needs him.

The terrace — shade, sea, and the sound of waves.

Where we gather, eat, and watch the sun rise.

Inside, looking out — the sea is always present.

Your room — simple, quiet, yours.

Evenings by the fire — slow conversation, warm light.

Maya · found in a storm · now the Heart of Il Sentiero.

The holder

Half Dutch,
half Italian,
fully arrived.

My name is Nikita. I grew up in the Netherlands, studied Social Work, and spent years working in psychiatry. I understood people's pain — but I knew there was a deeper layer that most approaches never reached.

I followed that knowing. It brought me here — to Salento, to the Land and the Sea, to this work. To the point inside every person where nothing exists and everything exists. Where stillness lives. Where everything can shift.

Il Sentiero means the path. I am not here to walk it for you. I am here to walk beside you — until you remember you always knew the way.

How I work

Presence.

There is a point inside every person where nothing exists and everything exists. Pure stillness. Pure potential. From that point, you can access anything. My work is to help you find your way there — and remove whatever stands between you and it.

01

Safe

Before anything can move, you need to feel safe enough to let it. I create that ground — without judgment, without rushing.

02

Slow

You have spent years being fast. Here, we slow down. Not as a method — but because slowness is the only language the deeper layers understand.

03

Reconnect

Not to something outside you. To the part of you that has always known. Your inner guidance. Your intuition. The stillness that has been waiting.

04

Move

What is ready to shift, shifts. Not because I push it — but because when you arrive at that still point inside, nothing that no longer serves you can stay.

When to come

The land sets the rhythm.

Now → 6 June 2026

The Retreats

Spring in Salento. The sea is cool and clear, the olive groves are green, and the days are long enough to go deep. A few spaces still open.

Mid July → End of August

The Still Season

The heat calls for slowness. Come and stay — a room, slow mornings, sea swims, gentle practice. Not a full retreat. A different kind of arrival.

End of August → Mid November

The Return

Autumn is the most potent season here. The land cools, the crowds leave, and the real work becomes possible again. Retreats resume in full.
